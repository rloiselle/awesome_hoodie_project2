Do you need an

Awesome Hoodie

to go with your favorite nerd shirt?

We do too! Which is why we launched the "Awesome Hoodie Project"!

Show Me The Hoodies! »

After hunting high and low, we found four great hoodies (& a few t-shirts) to fit different body types. We even found a hoodie to fit the lady nerds out there!

And here's the best part, Your hoodie or t-shirt does good things for the community!

A portion of each purchase will be donated to Code Scouts & Stumptown Syndicate to support coding education and community events!

Learn More »




Printing Options

There are four fantastic printing options for your awesome hoodie! And a few t-shirt options as well. Once you decide which hoodie (or t-shirt) fit & style you want, you will have the option to choose the logo you want printed on it.

Here is what the front & back of the printed hoodies will look like.

  • Portland Code School
  • Code Scouts
  • PDX.rb
  • Stumptown Syndicate



We love iconartistry, our awesome printer

We are working with iconartistry, a fantastic local printer that we love! By doing small runs of special custom prints just for us, iconartistry is making it possible for us to have awesome hoodies & donate to community organizations. How awesome are they?

An important note about your order:

Orders are filled in batches every 2 weeks.
You will receive an email notification when your order is received with a pick-up or shipping date.
Also, for now, all the hoodies will be a lovely charcoal/heather gray.

We welcome your feedback about any of this project.
You can email us at info@portlandcodeschool.com

Your Hoodie Can Help Make a Difference!





A portion of each hoodie purchase will be donated to Code Scouts & Stumptown Syndicate to support coding education and community events!

Since each hoodie is priced slightly differently, the donation amounts differ but the average donation amount for a hoodie works out to be about $8. Each donation amount from each purchase will be split evenly between these two great nonprofits!

Email us at info@portlandcodeschool.com with your questions, ideas, and comments!
